Injunctions

Representation in temporary and permanent injunction proceedings, including protective orders.

Free ConsultationCall (954) 459-4236

Protective Order Proceedings

Injunctions can impose serious restrictions quickly. Hearings may affect housing, family contact, firearm possession, employment, and future criminal exposure.

Preparing For The Hearing

Preparation may include text messages, call logs, witnesses, video, timelines, and cross-examination of the allegations.

Defense Or Petition Support

Hoffman Legal can assist people responding to injunction petitions and those who need guidance navigating protective-order proceedings.

How Hoffman Legal Can Help

  • Prepare evidence and witnesses for hearings
  • Address no-contact, firearm, housing, and family restrictions
  • Challenge unsupported or exaggerated allegations
  • Explain consequences before agreements are made

Slip & Fall Injuries

Claims involving wet floors, unsafe walkways, poor lighting, and ignored hazards.

Free ConsultationCall (954) 459-4236

Hazardous Property Conditions

Slip and fall claims may involve wet floors, uneven surfaces, poor lighting, debris, missing warnings, or neglected maintenance.

Notice And Responsibility

A key issue is whether the property owner or business knew or should have known about the hazard.

Evidence Can Disappear

Photos, incident reports, surveillance footage, witness names, and shoes/clothing should be preserved as early as possible.

How Hoffman Legal Can Help

  • Preserve evidence and document damages early
  • Communicate with insurance companies and opposing parties
  • Evaluate medical records, lost wages, and future needs
  • Pursue settlement or litigation when needed

DUI Defense

Focused defense for drunk driving allegations, license issues, breath tests, field sobriety tests, and DUI evidence.

Free ConsultationCall (954) 459-4236

DUI Evidence Review

DUI cases often turn on traffic-stop legality, officer observations, field sobriety exercises, breath or blood results, and whether testing procedures were followed.

License and Criminal Consequences

A DUI arrest can create separate administrative and criminal issues. Early action helps protect driving privileges and preserves defenses.

Building A Defense

The firm examines body camera video, breath-test maintenance, officer training, timing, and medical explanations that may affect the case.

How Hoffman Legal Can Help

  • Evaluate the stop, arrest, and testing procedures
  • Review breath, blood, urine, and field sobriety evidence
  • Address license consequences and court deadlines
  • Seek reductions, dismissals, or trial defenses where available
