Drug Possession Cases

Challenging searches, seizures, lab evidence, intent allegations, and possession charges.

Free ConsultationCall (954) 459-4236

Search And Seizure Issues

Drug possession cases frequently depend on whether law enforcement had lawful grounds to stop, search, seize, or question the accused.

Possession And Knowledge

The government must prove legal elements such as possession, knowledge, and sometimes intent. Shared vehicles, residences, or bags can create important factual disputes.

Alternative Resolutions

Depending on the facts and eligibility, diversion, treatment-based options, negotiated reductions, or dismissal strategies may be available.

How Hoffman Legal Can Help

  • Identify search-and-seizure defenses
  • Challenge possession, knowledge, and lab evidence
  • Explore diversion or negotiated outcomes where appropriate
  • Protect employment, education, and record-related interests
